Known for it's block parties, getting the invite to one shows that your no small fry in Night City's seedy underbelly. And if you don't have an invite, good luck getting in. Security is tougher than most Corps, thanks to the retinue of solos and netrunners renting there. While you might be likely to see daylight drug deals and hear an occasional shotting, Camden Court is one of the few places with a thriving community focus.
